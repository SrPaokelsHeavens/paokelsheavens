The Forbidden City had never been this quiet. Even the crickets seemed afraid to chirp near the walls of the Hall of Supreme Harmony.

Wei walked through the corridors like a ghost. Being a "Spirit-Rootless" slave had its advantages; most cultivators didn't even register his presence. He was part of the background, like the dust on the statues.

But today, the dust was different. It was vibrating with the echo of a forbidden technique.
